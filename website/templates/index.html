Fake news detection and mitigation

This site provides a fake news classification page and a fake news mitigation page. The user can input a news article, and our model will output a probability of it being true, along with a verdict regarding the veradicity of the article. The user can also input a tree-like propagation structure and our model will reveal the 10 most harmful nodes, that have the biggest probability of spreading the news.

Disclaimer: The classification result is output by a pre-trained model and should not be taken as ground-truth.
